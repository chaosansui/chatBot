## Debt Service Ratio (DSR)  

Total \(\mathsf{DSR} =\) [sum of monthly repayment for all debts (unsecured and secured monthly repayment amount) + monthly instalment amount of the new Funki's loan + sum of monthly living expenses] / monthly income.  

## Calculation of debts  

1. Credit card: (Total outstanding balance minus Loan-on-Card balance) x 4% plus Loan-on-Card instalment amount  

2. Revolving loan: 4% of outstanding balance  

3. Instalment loan: Monthly instalment  

4. Mortgage loan: Monthly instalment  

5. Car loan: Monthly instalment  

\*Any non- contributed trade lines declared by applicant OR observed from submitted documents  

(i.e. mortgage, commercial loan and other secured tradelines, short term loan for civil servant, credit union, student loan, no TU tradeline, etc.)  

## Living expense  

Such as MPF or Provident Fund, Rental expense, Mortgage expense, Others (i.e., education fee, car loan, credit union, etc.)
